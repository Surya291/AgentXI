Introducing Agent XI 

Been eagerly waiting for this IPL season's Fantasy to go live.

Starting a new experiment this season: handing full control of my fantasy team to my 
@openclaw
 / 
@NousResearch
 hermes agent.

It pulls live data from 
@ESPNcricinfo
  and keeps track of the season's developments like player form, injuries, availability, fixtures, matchups and decides everything on its own: the XI, captain, vc, transfers, the lot. Maybe along the way will also give it access to some YouTube creators suggesting fantasy strategies.

The interesting constraint is that you only get 160 transfers across the entire group stage. So it has to actually think about scarcity of swapping players and long-term value, not just pick the best XI every single match.

Curious to see how it does across the season. My intuition is it'll probably stay quiet early, hoard transfers, wait for a quarter or so of the season to pass and gather real data, then get aggressive and optimise transfers match by match.
