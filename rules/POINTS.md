# Fantasy League Points System

## Batting
- Per run: `1`
- Every four bonus: `1`
- Every six bonus: `2`
- 30 runs bonus: `4`
- Half century bonus: `8`
- Century bonus: `16`
- Duck dismissal (excluding bowlers): `-2`

## Bowling
- Wicket (except run-out): `25`
- Maiden over bonus: `12`
- LBW / Bowled bonus: `8`
- 3-wicket haul bonus: `4`
- 4-wicket haul bonus: `8`
- 5-wicket haul bonus: `16`

## Fielding
- Catch: `8`
- 3-catch bonus: `4`
- Stumping: `12`
- Run-out (direct): `12`
- Run-out (multiple players involved): `6`

## Economy Rate (minimum 2 overs bowled)
- Less than `5.00`: `6`
- `5.00` to `5.99`: `4`
- `6.00` to `6.99`: `2`
- `7.00` to `9.99`: `0`
- `10.00` to `10.99`: `-2`
- `11.00` to `11.99`: `-4`
- `12.00` and above: `-6`

## Strike Rate (except bowlers)
(minimum `10` balls played or `20` runs scored)
- Less than `50`: `-6`
- `50` to `59.99`: `-4`
- `60` to `69.99`: `-2`
- `70` to `129.99`: `0`
- `130` to `149.99`: `2`
- `150` to `169.99`: `4`
- `170` and above: `6`

## Others
- Captain: `x2`
- Vice-captain: `x1.5`
- In announced lineups: `4`

## Notes
- No points are awarded during a Super Over.
- Players scoring `100` runs (Century) receive points only for the Century bonus; no points are awarded for the Half-Century bonus.
- Overthrows: runs are credited to the batter on strike for that ball; if the overthrow results in a boundary, the batter does not receive Boundary Bonus points.
- Impact Player (IPL T20): points are counted only if the Impact Player is in your fantasy team (Playing XI).
